  • I remembered how to derive the formula for the roots of a quadratic :). Take any quadratic like $x^2 + ax + b$, then complete the square to get $(x+\frac{a}{2})^2 + (b- \frac{a^2}{4})$. Set this to zero and solve to get $$x=\pm\sqrt{\frac{a^2-4b}{4}}-\frac{a}{2}=\frac{-a\pm \sqrt{a^2-4b}}{2}.$$
